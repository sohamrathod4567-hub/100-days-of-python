# 🎮 Day 14 - Higher Lower Game

A fun command-line game built in Python where you compare two famous people, brands, or organizations and guess who has **more Instagram followers**.

This project was built as part of my **100 Days of Python** challenge.

---

## 📖 Overview

In this game, two random accounts are displayed along with their descriptions and countries.

Your goal is to guess which account has more followers by entering:

- **A** for Account A
- **B** for Account B

Each correct guess increases your score and brings in a new comparison. The game continues until you make an incorrect guess.
